# PrintFlow: Professional Print Shop Management System

PrintFlow is a premium business management system designed specifically for the unique needs of modern printing presses and boutique print shops. It transforms chaotic manual operations into a streamlined, digital workflow, allowing you to focus on growth rather than paperwork.

---

## 🚀 Core Value Propositions

### 1. From WhatsApp Chaos to Structured Orders
Avoid losing orders in WhatsApp threads or sticky notes. PrintFlow provides a **centralized order management system** where every job is tracked from the moment it's received to the final delivery.

### 2. GST Compliance Made Effortless
Calculating GST (CGST/SGST/IGST) manually is error-prone and time-consuming. PrintFlow **auto-generates GST-compliant invoices and challans** in seconds, ensuring your records are always audit-ready.

### 3. Professional Customer Experience
Impress your customers with **automated WhatsApp notifications**, professional PDF receipts, and a **self-service QR portal** where they can place orders and upload files directly.

---

## 🛠️ Feature Deep Dive

### 📦 Order & Workflow Management
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor jobs through stages: `Pending` → `In Progress` → `Ready` → `Delivered`.
- **Priority Handling**: Identify urgent orders at a glance.
- **Visual Proofing**: Upload and manage design proofs for customer approval.

### 📄 Intelligent Billing & GST
- **Instant Invoices**: Generate sequential, professional invoices with one click.
- **Challan Management**: Create delivery challans for large accounts.
- **GST Reports**: Export monthly data for easy tax filing.
- **Payment Tracking**: Track advances and outstanding balances per order.

### 🌐 Self-Service Customer Portal
- **QR Code Entry**: Customers scan a code in your shop to enter order details.
- **File Uploads**: Direct integration for receiving artwork.
- **Customer Ledger**: Every customer has a dedicated profile tracking their order history and total debt.

### 👥 Team & Operational Excellence
- **Attendance System**: Digital punch-in/out for staff management.
- **Leave Management**: Managed workflow for staff leave requests.
- **Inventory Integration**: Automated tracking of paper and materials as orders are processed.

### 📊 Business Insights
- **Revenue Analytics**: Real-time charts showing daily, weekly, and monthly growth.
- **Pending Receivables**: Instant list of who owes you money and how much.
- **Performance Metrics**: Identify your most profitable services and top customers.
